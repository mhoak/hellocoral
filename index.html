What is Coral?

For people who are creating something from nothing and want to be in a community that "get's it," Coral is home. Following our passions and turning them into viable companies is hard and lonely. It's not uncommon for friends and relatives to say "why don't you just go get a normal job?"

Coral is an apartment building launching in 2021 that is purpose built for community with programming that supports you on your journey from something to nothing.

What is Coral?

Coral is an apartment building and intentional community for people who are creating something out of nothing. Coral is the community for people who "get it" and understand just how hard it is to start from nothing. It's a space for us to live together in community and authentically sharing their highs and lows, successes and failures, fears and dreams, and inspiration.

Who is in this community?

From bakes to biotechs, from artists to architects whether you are a first timer or a seasoned entrepreneur with seveal companies under your belt, you are welcome at Coral. We believe that diversity breeds innovation.

Why Coral?

We are entrepreneurs, arthitects, founders, architects, software engineers, and we know how hard it can be to start from nothing and we are building the community that we have always wanted.
