We Are Originators

We call ourselves Originators. We are those who create something out of nothing. We toe the line between passion, dreams, and the drive to make them happen. We stand in the face of sometimes strong opposition to say, “I will make this happen!”

We are techies. We are artists. We are innovators. We are entrepreneurs. We are creators.

We create something out of nothing.

Out of nothing we come together now to build a community that is greater than the sum of its parts.

We are calling this community Coral. Like the physical ecosystem of the coral reef, Coral is the physical ecosystem for originators. We are creating living communities that promote, nurture, and inspire so we can thrive together.

Our first living ecosystem will be a 100 unit apartment building in Seattle and it will house people of diverse interests, backgrounds, and dreams who crave something different than the status quo, who crave a life of creating something out of nothing rather than a 9-5.

If that sounds like you, then we invite you to get in touch and say “hello.”
